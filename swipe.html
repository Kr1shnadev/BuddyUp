How to Use BuddyUp

Swipe Up to Accept - If you like a buddy's profile, swipe up on their card or click the green check button to send them a match request.
Swipe Down to Reject - If you're not interested, swipe down on their card or click the red X button to move to the next buddy.
Filter by Interests - Use the interest tags on the left to filter buddies based on your preferences in different categories.
